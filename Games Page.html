Star Wars: Rogue Squadron

This was my first ever game, modified from a template created by my professor during my first semester at BSU. I created the player and enemy models, added the stars and the Death Star in the background, and added a feature that would cause the player to loop to the other side of the playing field if they travelled too far. I also added the feature that if the player were to drop to a low enough hp level, they would be able to speed up by holding down the shift key to flee to safety. Though at the time I was well aware of the fact that this title was already copyrighted by Factor 5 and Lucasarts, this was my first ever game and I couldn't think of any better name for it. Given that this was my first ever game and would not be sold for profit, I didn't worry about using the same name.
